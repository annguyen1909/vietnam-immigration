## Where Can I Enter Vietnam with an eVisa?

**Travelers with a valid Vietnam eVisa can enter through designated international airports and major land border crossings.** The eVisa is valid for tourism and must be presented (preferably printed) with your passport at immigration.

## Overview: Using Your Vietnam eVisa

- The Vietnam eVisa is issued by Vietnam's immigration authorities.
- It is accepted at designated international airports and land borders listed below.
- Always carry a printed copy of your eVisa approval and your valid passport.

## Airports Accepting the Vietnam eVisa

You can enter Vietnam with an eVisa at the following international airports:

- **Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN) – Ho Chi Minh City:** Main gateway for southern Vietnam.
- **Noi Bai International Airport (HAN) – Hanoi:** Main gateway for northern Vietnam.
- **Da Nang International Airport (DAD) – Da Nang:** Central coast hub for Hoi An and Hue.
- **Cam Ranh International Airport (CXR) – Nha Trang:** Popular beach destination access.
- **Phu Quoc International Airport (PQC) – Phu Quoc Island:** Island resort gateway.

## Land Border Crossings

Vietnam eVisa holders may enter at major designated land border crossings with:

- **Cambodia** (e.g. Moc Bai, Bavet crossings)
- **Laos** (e.g. Lao Bao, Nam Can crossings)
- **China** (designated border gates as listed on your eVisa approval)

Always confirm your specific border gate is listed on your eVisa before travel.

## Arrival Process and Tips

1. Present your printed eVisa and passport at immigration.
2. Have your accommodation address ready if requested.
3. Keep your eVisa until departure—you may need it for hotel registration or exit.
