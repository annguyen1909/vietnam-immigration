## Overview: Family & Group eVisa Applications

Vietnam welcomes families and groups! The eVisa system allows you to apply for up to **15 travelers in a single group application**. This is ideal for families, friends, tour groups, or business delegations. Each traveler (including infants and children) must have their own eVisa, but you can submit all applications together for convenience and a single payment.

**Important:** Children also need visa applications. Families or groups can apply for up to 15 passengers in a single application.

## Who Can Apply Together?

- **Immediate Family:** Parents, children, spouses, legal guardians
- **Extended Family:** Grandparents, siblings, aunts, uncles, cousins (if traveling together)
- **Tour Groups:** Friends, school trips, sports teams, religious groups
- **Business Delegations:** Employees or associates attending conferences or meetings

**Note:** All travelers must be on the same itinerary and travel dates. Maximum: **15 travelers per group application**. For more than 15, submit multiple group applications.

## Step-by-Step Guide: Group Application (Up to 15 Travelers)

1. **Gather Information:**
   - Full names, passport details, birth dates, and contact info for all travelers
   - Relationship to group leader (e.g., parent, child, friend)
2. **Prepare Documents:**
   - Valid passport (6+ months from arrival) for each traveler
   - Recent passport photo for each traveler
   - Birth certificates for minors (if required)
   - Parental consent letters for children traveling with one parent/guardian
   - Proof of accommodation and travel itinerary
3. **Start Application:**
   - Go to the [Vietnam eVisa portal](/apply)
   - Select "Group/Family Application"
4. **Add Travelers:**
   - Enter details for each traveler (up to 15)
   - Upload required documents for each
5. **Review & Confirm:**
   - Double-check all information for accuracy
   - Ensure all documents are clear and legible
6. **Pay Fees:**
   - Pay government and service fees for all travelers in one transaction
   - Accepted payment: credit/debit card
7. **Submit Application:**
   - Submit the group application
   - Receive confirmation and reference numbers for each traveler
8. **Track Status:**
   - Use reference numbers to track each application
   - All approvals will be sent by email (typically within 3 hours to 3 working days)
9. **Print eVisas:**
   - Print each eVisa approval letter for border control

## Required Documents for Each Traveler

| Traveler | Passport | Photo | Birth certificate | Consent letter |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Adult** | Yes | Yes | No | No |
| **Child (both parents traveling)** | Yes | Yes | Recommended | No |
| **Child (one parent / guardian)** | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Infant** | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |

- **Proof of accommodation** (hotel booking, invitation letter)
- **Travel itinerary** (flights, dates, group leader contact)

## Special Cases: Children, Guardians, and Split Families

- **Children with One Parent:** Notarized consent letter from absent parent required
- **Children with Guardians:** Legal guardianship documents required
- **Split Families:** If family is split across two group applications, reference both applications in the notes
- **Adopted Children:** Adoption papers may be requested
- **Minors Traveling Alone:** Strongly discouraged; must have full documentation and consent

## Mixed Nationalities & Passport Types

- **Mixed Nationalities:** Each traveler applies based on their own nationality; some may require additional documents
- **Dual Citizens:** Use the passport you will travel with

## Payment, Fees, and Approval Process

- **Government Fee:** Varies by visa type (single entry: $55, multiple entry: $80 per person (tourist or business, 90 days))
- **Service Fee:** $59.99 per passenger
- **Payment:** Single transaction for all travelers
- **Processing Time:** Typically 3 hours to 3 working days

## Troubleshooting & Common Mistakes

- **Missing Documents:** Ensure all travelers have complete documents
- **Incorrect Information:** Double-check all names and passport numbers
- **Payment Issues:** Contact support if payment fails
- **Children Not Included:** Remember that children also need visa applications
