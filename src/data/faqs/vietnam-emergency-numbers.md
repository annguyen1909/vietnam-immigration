## Overview: Why Emergency Numbers Matter

Knowing the correct emergency numbers in Vietnam is essential for your safety and peace of mind, whether you are visiting as a tourist or living as a resident. Quick access to police, ambulance, and fire services can make a critical difference in urgent situations.

## Essential Emergency Numbers in Vietnam

| Service | Number | When to use |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Police** | 113 | Crime, safety threats, urgent police help |
| **Ambulance** | 115 | Medical emergencies nationwide |
| **Fire & rescue** | 114 | Fire, explosions, rescue situations |

## Specialized Hotlines & Support Services

- **Tourist support:** Contact your embassy or consulate for additional support in emergencies. Keep your embassy's contact details handy.
- **Medical facilities:** Major hospitals in Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, and Da Nang offer 24/7 emergency care.

## How to Use Emergency Numbers in Vietnam

1. Dial the appropriate number from any phone (mobile or landline).
2. State your location clearly—street name, district, and nearest landmark if possible.
3. English may be limited outside major cities; ask a local to assist if needed.

## Tips for Travelers and Expats

- Save **113**, **115**, and **114** in your phone before you travel.
- Carry your passport and eVisa printout when exploring.
- For non-emergency medical issues, visit international clinics in major tourist areas.
