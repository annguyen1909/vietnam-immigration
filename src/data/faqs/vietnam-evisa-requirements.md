## Overview

The Vietnam eVisa is an official electronic travel authorization for foreign nationals wishing to visit Vietnam for tourism and travel. The process is fully online and designed to be accessible for travelers worldwide. This guide covers every aspect of the application, from eligibility to troubleshooting.

## Who Needs a Vietnam eVisa?

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Vietnam. The eVisa is available for tourism and travel. Some nationalities are visa-exempt or must apply at a Vietnamese embassy—check the [official requirements page](/check-requirement) for your country.

**Embassy-Only Countries:**

- Some countries may require embassy applications for certain visa types

## Eligibility by Nationality

- **eVisa Eligible:** Most countries in Europe, Asia, the Americas, Africa, and Oceania
- **Visa-Exempt:** Check official Vietnam immigration website
- **Embassy-Only:** Some countries may require embassy applications

## Types of Vietnam eVisas

- **Tourist eVisa (Single Entry for 90 days)** – $55
- **Tourist eVisa (Multiple Entries for 90 days)** – $80
- **Business eVisa (Single Entry for 90 days)** – $55
- **Business eVisa (Multiple Entries for 90 days)** – $80

Note: Families or groups can apply for up to 15 passengers in a single application. Children also need visa applications.

## Required Documents for All Applicants

1. **Valid Passport:** At least 6 months validity from arrival date
2. **Recent Passport Photo:** Color, clear background
3. **Travel Itinerary:** Flight details, accommodation booking
4. **Email Address:** For eVisa approval and updates
5. **Payment Method:** Credit/debit card
6. **Supporting Documents:** Depending on visa type (see below)

## Special Requirements by Visa Type

- **Tourist (single/multiple, 90 days):** Tourism, family visits, leisure
- **Business (single/multiple, 90 days):** Meetings, conferences, commercial visits
- **Minors:** Birth certificate, parental consent letter if traveling with one parent/guardian
- **Family Visit:** Invitation letter from host in Vietnam, copy of host's ID
- **Medical Treatment:** Medical certificate, hospital invitation

## Step-by-Step Application Process

1. **Check Eligibility:** Use the [requirements tool](/check-requirement)
2. **Prepare Documents:** Passport, photo, itinerary, and any special documents
3. **Apply Online:** Complete the application at [Vietnam eVisa portal](/apply)
4. **Pay Fees:** Pay government and service fees
5. **Upload Documents:** Attach all required files
6. **Track Status:** Use your reference number to check progress
7. **Receive eVisa:** Approval sent by email (typically within 5 business days)
8. **Print eVisa:** Bring a printed copy for border control

## Photo & Document Guidelines

- **Photo:** Color, recent, no hats/glasses, white background
- **Passport Scan:** Clear, full page, no glare
- **File Types:** JPG, PNG, or PDF
- **File Size:** Under 2MB per file

## Real-World Scenarios & Edge Cases

### Scenario 1: Family of Four (Two Adults, Two Children)

- Each person must apply (children also need visas)
- Families or groups can apply for up to 15 passengers in a single application
- Children need birth certificates and parental consent
- All documents must be uploaded for each applicant

### Scenario 2: Extended Stay Traveler

- Choose the 90-day tourist eVisa for longer stays
- Ensure passport has sufficient validity

### Scenario 3: Short Trip Traveler

- Choose the 90-day tourist eVisa variant that matches your entry needs (single or multiple)
- Perfect for quick getaways, beach trips, and temple visits

### Scenario 4: Medical Emergency

- Medical certificate and hospital invitation
- Expedited processing may be possible

### Scenario 5: Passport Expiring Soon

- Application denied; must renew passport before applying

### Scenario 6: Name Mismatch

- Provide legal documents to explain discrepancies

## Troubleshooting & Common Mistakes

- **Photo Rejected:** Use a recent, clear photo
- **Passport Expiring Soon:** Renew before applying
- **Missing Documents:** Double-check all uploads
- **Wrong Email:** Double-check for typos
- **Payment Declined:** Try another card or contact your bank
- **Slow Processing:** Processing typically takes within 5 business days
- **File Size Too Large:** Compress files before uploading
- **Untranslated Documents:** Provide certified translations
- **Application Denied:** Review reasons, correct, and reapply
