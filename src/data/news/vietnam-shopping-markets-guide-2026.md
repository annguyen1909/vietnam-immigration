Shopping in Vietnam is a **conversation** as much as a transaction. Markets price in **VND**; carry **small bills** for snacks and water while keeping larger notes separate. Tote a **foldable bag**, photograph fragile wraps for insurance, and treat opening quotes as starting points—not final facts.

## Ben Thanh Market, Ho Chi Minh City

**Ben Thanh** blends apparel, dried fruit, pepper, and lacquerware under one roof—cooler midday than perimeter pavement stalls.

### Smart buys and benchmarks

- **Dak Lak robusta**, **Phuc Long–style** tea gifts, **Saigon cinnamon**
- Compare supermarket shelves nearby before haggling
- Evening perimeter stalls skew street-food—watch bags after **18:00**

| Item | Opening stall band (VND) |
| --- | --- |
| T-shirt | 120,000–250,000 |
| Coffee 250 g (labelled) | 150,000–350,000 |
| Lacquer plate (small) | 300,000–800,000 |

## Dong Xuan Market, Hanoi

**Dong Xuan** skews **wholesale** downstairs—fabric, notions, bulk candy—while fringe alleys sell **propaganda prints**, scarves, and winter wear before **Sapa** trips.

- Compare **three stalls** before committing
- Lift ceramics to check glaze consistency
- Bubble wrap often **15,000–50,000 VND**—patient vendors help

## Hoi An Night Market

**Nguyen Hoang** lanes fill with lantern stalls, coconut-leaf crafts, and grilled seafood aromas.

- **Tailoring rush orders**: verify seam allowances and fitting times
- Leather sandals: inspect stitching under phone light
- Bargain politely after you intend to buy; respect **no-photo** signs on living vendors

## Quality control and etiquette

| Check | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Vacuum-packed food dates | Gifts and customs safety |
| Coffee origin labels | Avoid opaque blends |
| Wood/bamboo crafts | Some countries restrict untreated imports |
| Ask before portraits | Courtesy in wholesale aisles |

Decline redundant plastic—mesh totes survive humidity better.

## Budget planning in VND

Street T-shirts might open **120,000–250,000 VND**; hand-painted lanterns cost more by size. Keep a **photo price note** on your phone when fatigue invites impulse inflation.

### Midday hotel drop strategy

Store ceramics and textiles before evening food crawls—porters and crowded buses thank you.

## VAT refunds and shipping

Save receipts when participating retailers offer **VAT refund** counters at major airports. Shipping internationally requires honest customs declarations for crafts and wood products.
