## Choosing a coast for your 2026 holiday

| Destination | Vibe | Best for |
|-------------|------|----------|
| Da Nang | City-beach hybrid | Families, first-timers, Marble Mountains |
| Nha Trang | High-energy bay | Resorts, island snorkel day trips |
| Mui Ne | Wind and dunes | Kitesurfing, photography |
| Phu Quoc | Island sunsets | Long beach stays, night markets |

## Da Nang’s city-beach balance

**My Khe** offers broad sands minutes from **Marble Mountains** lifts and contemporary cafés. Sunrise swims help jet lag; weekend evenings bring **Dragon Bridge** fire displays.

### Practical Da Nang tips

- Use marked crosswalks—motorbike streams move fast between towers and the airport corridor.
- **Son Tra Peninsula** viewpoints suit helmeted scooter loops when confident in traffic.
- Street meals near the beach often run **50,000–120,000 VND** per person.

## Nha Trang’s energetic bay

High-rise resorts shoulder **Tran Phu**’s palm strip while boats dart toward offshore islands for snorkel picnics. **Po Nagar Cham towers** anchor cultural mornings before sunscreen afternoons.

Rainy-season spikes occasionally muddy shallows—monitor forecasts before charter deposits. Two-tank dive days cluster here; see our diving guide if scuba is on your list.

## Mui Ne’s wind, dunes, and fairy stream

Red-and-white **sand dunes** photograph best at dawn; afternoon thermal winds attract **kitesurfers** beside fishing fleets. Resorts west of **Ham Tien** split between quiet lanes and beach-bar playlists—read reviews for noise.

**Fairy Stream (Suoi Tien)** wading needs grippy soles on slick clay. Kite lessons often quote **800,000–2,000,000 VND** per session depending on gear and instructor tier.

## Phu Quoc’s western sunsets

Long beaches invite barefoot sundowns; inland rides reveal **pepper plantations** and fish-sauce warehouses. Domestic flights pair with **Ho Chi Minh City** gateways—leave buffer nights around tropical storms.

Night **squid fishing** charters tempt insomniacs; motion-sickness remedies still help on gentle Gulf swells.

## Coastal budgeting cues in VND

- Cash simplifies **seafood-by-weight** stalls; confirm totals before cooking.
- Resort minibars price aggressively—street smoothies rescue overheated afternoons for **20,000–40,000 VND**.
- Tipping housekeeping **20,000–50,000 VND** per night is appreciated, not required.
- Sunscreen and reef-safe lotions cost less in city pharmacies—stock up before islands.

## Family-friendly timing

Shoulder months blend manageable school absences with thinner airport queues—still watch **Vietnamese school holiday peaks** when domestic flights fill. Flagged swim zones matter for children; limestone channels can hide currents.

## Sun care and sustainability

Reapply **broad-spectrum lotion** after swims; rinse snorkel gear away from coral mouths when hopping between cities. Refuse redundant plastic bags at fruit stalls—mesh totes survive salty breezes.

Leave sandals outside temple thresholds when afternoon sightseeing interrupts beach days—humidity climbs fast inland.

Confirm **entry approval** before non-refundable resort deposits, then let waves set your clock.
