Vietnam packs bold contrasts into short distances: **misty karsts**, **mirror rice ponds**, and **heritage streets** that glow after dark. This 2026 guide covers **Ha Long Bay**, **Hoi An**, and **Sapa**—the tripod trio most travellers combine—plus kit, permits, and ethical portrait habits priced in honest **VND**.

## Ha Long Bay and Lan Ha: karst drama

**Ha Long Bay** (and quieter **Lan Ha** routes from **Cat Ba**) peaks at **dawn** when fog softens peaks and deck crowds thin.

### Lens and exposure notes

- **24–70mm** handles most boat compositions; telephoto isolates pillars
- **Polariser** cuts haze on midday transfers
- **Kayak-low angles** add depth—coordinate timing with operators

Overnight cruises (**several million VND** mid-range cabins in peak season) buy two sunrises; compare passenger caps and engine-noise policies before booking.

## Hoi An Ancient Town: lanterns and symmetry

**Hoi An** shines **30–60 minutes after sunset** when silk lanterns ignite and the **Thu Bon River** holds long exposures.

### Day and night workflow

| Time | Subject | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 05:30–07:00 | Ochre walls, empty alleys | Beat tour buses |
| Golden hour | Tran Phu facades | Pastel backgrounds |
| After dark | Bridges, river reflections | Tripods—keep walkways clear |

Private courtyards may charge **small VND photography fees**—pay when requested.

## Sapa and northern terraces: lines and mist

**Sapa** plus **Lao Chai–Ta Van** deliver graphic terraces in telephoto compression.

### Seasonal colour (approximate)

- **Electric green**: often **April–June**
- **Harvest gold**: often **September–early October**
- **Buffer days** for cloud—weather shifts fast

Rain and mud are real: **gaiters**, **lens wipes**, and **waterproof boots** matter. Ethical access beats staging: cooperative guides, agreed **VND** fees, no exploitative setups.

## Practical kit and permits

Moisture and dust punish gear—zip-lock sensor swabs help. For **commercial or drone** work, verify **national park and municipal rules**; keep cruise tickets and park receipts for bag checks.
